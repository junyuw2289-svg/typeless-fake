Meeting Notes — Product Review

schedule Feb 20, 2026 at 2:34 PM timer 3 min 42 sec text_fields 847 words Slack

We discussed the Q1 product roadmap during today's meeting. The key priorities are as follows:

1. Voice Recognition Improvements
The team agreed to prioritize multi-language support, starting with Mandarin Chinese and Japanese. Sarah will lead the integration with our new speech model, targeting a 15% improvement in accuracy for non-English languages.

2. App Integration Expansion
We're adding support for Linear and Notion in the next sprint. The API connectors are ready; we just need to finalize the UI for permission management. David mentioned that the OAuth flow should be standardized across all integrations.

3. Performance Optimization
Current transcription latency is around 1.8 seconds for a 30-second clip. The goal is to bring it down to under 1 second by switching to our streaming pipeline. This will require changes to both the recording buffer and the API client.

Action Items:

  • Sarah: Finalize multi-language model integration by Feb 28
  • David: Standardize OAuth flow documentation
  • Jun: Benchmark streaming pipeline performance
  • Next review: March 6, 2026
